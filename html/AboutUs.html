Our Stories

C’Groovy was founded in 2016 with a vision to revolutionize the way people experience music. Starting as a small startup with a passion for sound, we have grown into a renowned music streaming service, offering millions of songs across various genres and artists worldwide.

2018

Expansion into Global Streaming

After gaining popularity in the local market, C’Groovy expanded its music catalog by partnering with record labels and artists worldwide. The introduction of personalized playlists and AI-driven recommendations helped users discover new music effortlessly.

2020

Mobile App Launch

With the rise of mobile streaming, C’Groovy launched its iOS and Android apps, making it easier for users to enjoy music anytime, anywhere. The app featured offline listening, high-quality audio streaming, and user-generated playlists.

2024

Digital Business Transformation

In response to the rapid growth of digital technology, C’Groovy decided to strengthen its online presence by building a dedicated company website. This website will serve as a hub for discovering music, artist collaborations, and engaging music enthusiasts worldwide.
